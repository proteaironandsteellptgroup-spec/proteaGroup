About Protea Iron and Steel Group

Founded in 1970 as a five-person mining team, Protea has grown into a fully integrated global metals and mining company serving 121 countries and regions.

The Group supplies military-grade, medical-grade, and aerospace-grade aluminum, steel, zinc, nickel, copper, and over 30 strategic metals.

Protea maintains offices in New York, California, Washington, London, Munich, Berlin, Cape Town, Johannesburg, and Durban.

Mining operations span South Africa, Namibia, Botswana, Zambia, Chile, and Australia.

With over 14,000 employees, annual output includes 170,000 tonnes of aluminum, 1.42 million tonnes of steel, and 140,000 tonnes of copper.
