Building a Global Metals Enterprise Since 1970

Who We Are

Founded in 1970 as a five-person mining operations team, Protea Iron and Steel Group has evolved into a fully integrated global metals and mining enterprise with capabilities spanning exploration, extraction, refining, processing, logistics, and international distribution.

Today, the Group supplies high-quality military-grade, medical-grade, and aerospace-grade materials to customers across 121 countries and regions worldwide. Our diversified portfolio covers more than 30 strategic metal categories, including aluminum, steel, zinc, nickel, and copper.

Global Presence

Protea maintains regional offices and commercial operations in New York, California, and Washington (United States); London (United Kingdom); Munich and Berlin (Germany); as well as Cape Town, Johannesburg, and Durban (South Africa).

Mining and processing assets are strategically located across South Africa, Botswana, Namibia, Zambia, Chile, and Australia, supporting resilient global supply chains.

Scale & Capability

With approximately 14,000 employees worldwide, Protea operates industry-leading annual production capacity, including 170,000 tonnes of specialty aluminum, 1.42 million tonnes of steel products, and 140,000 tonnes of copper.

Through disciplined capital allocation, operational excellence, and a strong commitment to safety and sustainability, Protea continues to strengthen its position among the world’s leading metals and mining groups.
