Who We Are

Protea Iron and Steel Group is a diversified industrial group focused on responsible steel processing, resource supply, and long-term value creation across global markets.

Our operations are built on safety, sustainability, disciplined capital allocation, and long-standing partnerships with customers across infrastructure, manufacturing, and energy sectors.

Our Operations

Steel Processing

Advanced processing and distribution of high-grade steel products serving construction, manufacturing, and industrial applications.

Energy & Resources

Supporting energy-intensive industries through efficient sourcing, logistics coordination, and long-term supply relationships.

Infrastructure Supply

Delivering materials and solutions for large-scale infrastructure projects across emerging and developed markets.
