About Us

Founded in 1970 as a five-person mining operations team, Protea Iron and Steel Group has grown into a fully integrated global metals and mining enterprise.

Today, the Group supplies high-quality military-grade, medical-grade, and aerospace-grade materials to customers across 121 countries and regions worldwide. Our portfolio spans more than 30 metal categories, including aluminum, steel, zinc, nickel, and copper, covering the full value chain from exploration and extraction to refining, processing, logistics, and global distribution.

Protea maintains offices in New York, California, and Washington (United States); London (United Kingdom); Munich and Berlin (Germany); as well as Cape Town, Johannesburg, and Durban (South Africa).

Our mining assets are strategically located across South Africa, Botswana, Namibia, Zambia, Chile, and Australia, ensuring secure, diversified, and long-term supply.

With approximately 14,000 employees worldwide, Protea operates industry-leading annual production capacity, including 170,000 tonnes of specialty aluminum, 1.42 million tonnes of steel products, and 140,000 tonnes of copper. Our scale, quality standards, and operational discipline place the Group among global leaders in the metals industry.
